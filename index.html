    Current Work

    I currently works on exploring the neural circuit plasticity in the early visual cortex as a result of familiarity learning with video stimulus. This research seeks to answer whether neural connectivity in the early visual cortex can encode spatial temporal memories with horizontal connections, leading to familiarity suppression. I am also investigating the familiarity effect in neural networks by examining how the neural responses in a hierarchical neural network compare to physiological data when exposed to familiar stimuli. In addition, I also used dimensionality reduction methods on neural data to explore the evolution of neural trajectories in lower-dimensional manifolds in a monkey observing familiar data.
